About Ryal Motors

Trading hall with an area of ​​10 thousand sq. meters can accommodate more than 300 cars, prestigious brands Bentley, Ferrari, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, armored, retro and electro cars. A large assortment and a competent approach of the personnel based on the professionalism and saving of the Client's time are our hallmarks. An urgent assessment, legal assistance, technical support, and all this quickly, without unnecessary delays, with a guarantee of benefits for all parties to the transaction. We are always ready to help in buying and selling a car. With us you can buy elite cars on favorable terms, put your car on a commission at your price, urgently sell the vehicle with an assessment on favorable terms. Cars are our passion. To everyone who wants to share this passion with us, we invite you to participate in our Royal Motors Club. We are glad to see you in our showroom. Contact us!
